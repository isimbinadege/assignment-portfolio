About Me

My journey into coding began with a simple curiosity and a love for solving problems. I’ve always enjoyed breaking down challenges and finding creative solutions, and that passion naturally led me to the world of programming. Over time, I discovered how exciting it is to turn ideas into real, interactive digital products. Today, I specialize in Frontend Development and UI/UX Design, where I combine technical skills with creativity to build clean, modern, and user-friendly experiences. For me, coding isn’t just a career — it’s a way to create, innovate, and make an impact.

I hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Information Technology from the University of Rwanda, where I built a strong foundation in both technology and business principles. Before that, I studied Software Development in secondary school, which gave me my first hands-on experience with programming and sparked my passion for creating digital solutions. This blend of academic knowledge and practical experience has equipped me with both the technical expertise and the problem-solving mindset to thrive as a developer and designer.
